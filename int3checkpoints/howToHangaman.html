How I Made the Hangman Game

Intro

For a project in my computer science class, I was required to make a program that could solve a problem with the knowledge we gained in the most recent unit. In my case, I just knew I wanted to make a hangman game, because I knew it was possible given what we had just learned. Unfrtunately, I couldn't think of a porblem that hangman would solve. Thankfully, my friend Archer Prochazka pointed out that the Hangman game actually solves the problem of young children not knowing how to spell and makes spelling exciting. Because of this excuse, I was able to keep make hangman like I planned. You can explore my game by clicking the link in the avbar, and after that, you can revisit this page and continue to look at my explanation of how I made it.
